## Overview
A premium romantic SaaS login direction for Ola Brand Design. The atmosphere is cinematic, dark, emotional, and controlled: heartbeat motion, red-pink bloom, glass surfaces, and restrained authentication UI.

## Colors
Use a near-black burgundy base, rose highlights, and pale blush bloom. Avoid candy pinks, pure black, or saturated purple gradients.

## Typography
Display type uses Cormorant Garamond for an editorial romantic tone. Interface and form text use Manrope for modern SaaS clarity.

## Layout
Use a split hero/login composition on desktop and a stacked layout on mobile. Keep the login card constrained near 500px wide for readability.

## Elevation & Depth
Depth comes from translucent glass layers, radial bloom, subtle noise, and soft rose shadows. Avoid hard drop shadows or plastic-looking glows.

## Shapes
Large rounded cards and pill badges create softness. Inputs keep 16px radius and 56px minimum height for touch comfort.

## Components
- Heart orb: transform-only pulse with soft bloom and no exaggerated bounce.
- Signal strip: small rhythmic bars echoing heartbeat UI.
- Login card: glassmorphism surface with label-above-input fields and visible focus states.
- Primary button: warm rose-to-blush gradient with subtle lift and pressed feedback.

## Do's and Don'ts
Do keep motion slow, smooth, and emotional. Do respect reduced motion. Do use transform and opacity for looped effects. Don't use childish hearts, cartoon icons, fake placeholder content, or abrupt transitions.
